How to Play

The computer has devised a secret code made up of four out a possible six fruits.

🍋 🍐 🍉 🍓 🍌 🍒

There are no duplications within the code. Your objective is to guess the code within 10 tries.

You make your choice using the dropdown arrows and then press the submit button. If your guess is incorrect, you will receive a score as follows:

⚫ means a fruit in the right place and ⚪ means a fruit in the wrong place

(but still part of the code.) You get nothing for fruit that are not in the code.


Keep guessing until you get it right or run out of tries.



            🍒🍋 Fruit Code Breaker 🍋🍒

This game is based on the board game Mastermind which was invented in 1970 by Mordecai Meirowitz of Israel and was, itself, based on a paper-based game called Bulls and Cows.
